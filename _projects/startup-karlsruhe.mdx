[Startup Karlsruhe](http://startup-karlsruhe.de) is a small German site that provides an overview of the startup ecosystem in Karlsruhe.
I built it as a side project during my time as President of [PionierGarage](https://pioniergarage.de), Germany's biggest student entrepreneurship club.
