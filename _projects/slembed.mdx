slembed is a drop-in proxy for OpenAI-compatible embedding APIs that reduces token spend by up to 50% without changing your model, code, or vector database. It optimizes text before it hits the embedding model through smart preprocessing, quality-based pipeline routing, and built-in caching, with per-request quality thresholds so you can dial cost vs. quality per use case.
