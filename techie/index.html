Programming and Computer Science

I am a techie, computer scientist, and researcher.

Research on human behavior in social networks

During my time at university, I have published three papers: Two about human behavior in online social networks and one on integrating a database encryption into the Database Abstraction Layer (DBAL). I wrote my bachelor thesis about a concept very similar to Cambridge Analytica because I built an app that kind of tricked users into giving their permission to access Facebook API data. My masters thesis was about the impact of personality on the behavior of programmers. I performed a study with over 1000 developers that found significant impacts of personality on programmers' behavior. For example, we could show that programmers with specific traits prefer specific languages, libraries, stacks, and tools.
